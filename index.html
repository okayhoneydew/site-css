A CLOSER LOOK

Heidi first came to me thinking she just wanted me to produce a song for her. It was already written, she had performed it live a few times, and had some ideas about where she wanted the production go…

But as I started working on the track…I struggled with finding a direction that felt “right”

When that starts to happen, I usually start asking “Is something going wrong with the writing?”

So, I started picking at the first draft of lyrics…& I felt like there was a disconnect somewhere…

The First Demo

Here's a clip from the very first demo she sent me. Lyrics are somewhere around here too.

The Airport V.1

Verse

I watch the sunset as we touch down

I see the bags come out as people gather around

The tears the warm embrace of hellos

the pain of goodbyes, the hope in human race

then the noise turns down

Chorus

when I see you...you

It's been a while since I shared my heart

but now there's you

now...this is by no means "bad"

I definitely see what she went for here. 


  • Flight just landed
  • we’re being greeted by someone
  • probably just got into a relationship with them
  • So we have an uplifting, positive little story


Obviously great vocal tone, piano is nice...


…but I have more questions…


Why “The Airport?” Did you guys meet there? What was life like before meeting? Why should I care about this “you” person? How are they significant? Are they even that significant at all?


I just felt like there were 2 topics competing with each other, and that was the disconnect for me. I knew there was an opportunity to re-work this song a bit with her.


I wouldn't be doing my job fully if I just decided to continue on with production.  I needed to get Heidi to answer the golden question:

WHAT ARE YOU REALLY TRYING TO SAY?

Not always simple to answer. If you watched the video above, you know that it was a struggle at times. (More on that below)
